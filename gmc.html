GMC Models

About GMC

GMC (General Motors Truck Company) was founded in Detroit in the early-1900s by Max Grabowsky. Known for its durable utility vehicles and trucks, GMC specializes in SUVs and pickups like the Sierra Denali and Yukon XL. The brand is positioned as a premium offering alongside Chevrolet under General Motors.

Models

Sierra HD

The 2024 GMC Sierra HD is a heavy-duty truck designed for extreme power and off-road capability. With up to 975 lb-ft of torque, a 2-inch lift, and advanced safety features, it’s perfect for both work and adventure. The infotainment system includes a 13.4-inch touchscreen with Google services built-in.

Canyon AT4X

The Canyon AT4X is a mid-size pickup tailored for off-road adventures. It features a high-output engine with 310 horsepower and body armor from American Expedition Vehicles (AEV). With heated seats, advanced infotainment, and rugged tires, it’s built for comfort and capability.

Yukon Denali XL

The Yukon Denali XL is a full-size luxury SUV offering spacious interiors and premium features. It combines rugged performance with advanced technology, making it ideal for families or long road trips.

Terrain SLT AWD

The Terrain SLT AWD is a compact SUV that balances style, comfort, and practicality. With all-wheel drive capability and modern tech features like Apple CarPlay integration, it’s perfect for urban driving or weekend getaways.

Acadia AT4 AWD

The Acadia AT4 AWD is a mid-size SUV built for adventure. It features off-road capabilities, all-wheel drive, and a spacious interior with premium materials. Perfect for families who enjoy exploring the great outdoors.
