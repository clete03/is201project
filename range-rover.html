About Range Rover

The Range Rover line was launched by Land Rover in 1970 under British Leyland. Known for its luxury and off-road capability, Range Rover has become a global standard for premium SUVs. The brand is now part of Jaguar Land Rover and continues to innovate with models like the Velar and Autobiography.
